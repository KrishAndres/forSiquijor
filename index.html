Discover Pristine Beaches

Siquijor's coastline is adorned with powdery white sand beaches and crystal-clear waters that invite you to leave your worries behind. Imagine lounging under the sun at Paliton Beach, with its serene and picturesque setting, often referred to as the "Little Boracay" of Siquijor. The fine white sand and calm, shallow waters make it an ideal spot for sunbathing, leisurely swims, and capturing breathtaking sunsets. If adventure calls your name, head to Salagdoong Beach. Known not only for its pristine white sand and clear waters but also for its thrilling cliff diving spots, this beach offers an exhilarating experience.

For those seeking solitude, Kagusuan Beach is a hidden gem waiting to be discovered. With unique rock formations and crystal-clear waters, this secluded beach provides an intimate escape from the crowds, allowing you to connect with nature's beauty in peace. And don't miss the Tubod Marine Sanctuary, a protected area near Coco Grove Beach Resort. This underwater paradise is perfect for snorkeling and diving, where vibrant coral reefs and diverse marine life await your exploration.

Salagdoong Beach

The beach is surrounded by lush greenery and limestone cliffs, providing a dramatic backdrop for an exciting beach day. Whether you're diving off the cliffs or simply relaxing by the shore, Salagdoong Beach promises a memorable experience.

Paliton Beach

Often referred to as the "Little Boracay" of Siquijor, Paliton Beach offers a serene and picturesque setting. Its fine white sand and calm, shallow waters make it an ideal spot for sunbathing, leisurely swims, and taking in breathtaking sunsets.

Cantabon Cave

An exhilarating spelunking experience awaits in this cave, with its impressive stalactites and stalagmites. Navigate through narrow passages and marvel at the natural formations that have been sculpted over millennia.

Tubod Marine Sanctuary

The sanctuary is home to vibrant coral reefs and a diverse array of marine life, making it a top spot for underwater exploration. Snorkeling in the crystal-clear waters allows you to witness the colorful underwater world and appreciate the island's marine biodiversity.

Mt. Bandilaan

The highest point on the island, offering panoramic views and lush hiking trails. As you trek through the Bandilaan National Park, you'll encounter diverse flora and fauna, making it a nature lover's paradise.

Getting Around Siquijor

Navigating the enchanting island of Siquijor is an adventure in itself, offering various transportation options that cater to every kind of traveler. From scenic rides to intimate explorations, here's how you can get around and experience the island's magic:

  • Habal-Habal (Motorbike Taxi): Price: Approximately ₱50-₱100 ($0.86-$1.72) per ride, depending on the distance.
  • Tricycle: Price: Approximately ₱10-₱20 ($0.17-$0.34) per person for short distances, or ₱200-₱500 ($3.45-$8.62) for longer trips.
  • Jeepney/Easyride: Price: Approximately ₱10-₱20 ($0.17-$0.34) per person, depending on the distance.
  • Bicycle: Price: Approximately ₱100-₱200 ($1.72-$3.45) per day.
  • Walking: Price: Free!
